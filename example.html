ClimaFacts Stats (Example)

This web page provide dynamic statistics about the ClimaFacts Knowledge Graph

ClimaFactsKG uses sc:ClaimReview from the Schema.org vocabulary to represent claims and scientific corrections collected from the Skeptical Science website. The categorisation of misinforming climate claims is based on the CARDS taxonomy. CARDS is used to connect sc:ClaimReview between ClimaFactsKG and CimpleKG.

RDF Namespaces

The ClimaFactsKG namespace is: https://purl.net/climafactskg/ns#.

ClimaFactsKG commonly uses the following namespaces and prefixes:
